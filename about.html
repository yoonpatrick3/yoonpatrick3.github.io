Who is Patrick Yoon?

Born in California and raised in Chicago, I am a Korean American student attending Northeastern University. As of now, I am currently studying computer science with a minor in business anaytics. My computer science concentration lies in machine learning and artificial intelligence. I started programming at a young age, creating fun little websites using the Notepad app and meaningless plugins in Minecraft. I started to take programming more seriously when I took AP Computer Science in my sophomore year of high school. Through the years, I have learned about web/mobile development, machine learning, natural language processing, and scripting. I have also dabbled in cryptography and UI/UX design.


Outside of computer science, I enjoy playing video games such as League of Legends and Smash Ultimate. I am involved in Northeastern's Korean American Student Association as their treasurer, responsible for handling any of the organization's financials. I also like to listen to Korean music and watch Korean shows and dramas.
